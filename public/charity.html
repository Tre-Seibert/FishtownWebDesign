Free Websites for Non-Profits

What's Included

Everything You Need for a Professional Online Presence

Our free website program includes the same high-quality, hand-coded websites we build for our paying clients. You'll get a fast, mobile-responsive website that helps your non-profit reach more people and make a bigger impact.

  • Custom Hand-Coded Website

    Up to 5 pages of custom content, designed specifically for your non-profit's mission and goals. No templates, no WordPress—just clean, fast code.

  • Mobile Responsive Design

    Your website will look perfect on all devices—desktop, tablet, and mobile. Most of your visitors will be on mobile, so we make sure it works flawlessly.

  • Fast Loading Speeds

    Our websites load in under 1 second, ensuring visitors stay engaged and don't bounce away. Fast websites also rank better in Google search results.

  • Basic SEO Optimization

    We'll optimize your website for search engines so people can find your non-profit when they search for services you provide or causes you support.

  • Free Hosting (First Year)

    We'll host your website for free for the first year. After that, hosting is just $20/month—much less than you'd pay elsewhere for the same quality.

  • Simple Content Updates

    Need to update your website? Just send us an email and we'll make the changes for you. We want to make it easy for you to keep your site current.

Eligibility

Who Qualifies for a Free Website?

We want to help as many non-profits as possible, but we have limited capacity. To ensure we're helping organizations that truly need it, we have a few simple requirements.

  • Registered Non-Profit Status

    Your organization must be a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit or have equivalent non-profit status recognized by the IRS.

  • Serves Philadelphia Community

    Priority is given to organizations that serve the Philadelphia area, though we consider non-profits from surrounding regions as well.

  • Limited Web Development Budget

    This program is designed for non-profits that don't have the resources to invest in professional web design. We prioritize organizations with limited budgets.

  • Clear Mission & Goals

    Your organization should have a clear mission statement and specific goals for how a website will help further your cause.

How It Works

The Application Process

Applying for a free website is simple. We review applications on a rolling basis and select organizations that align with our mission of supporting the Philadelphia community.

  • 1

    Submit Your Application

    Fill out our contact form and let us know about your non-profit, your mission, and why a website would help your organization make a bigger impact.

  • 2

    We Review Your Application

    Our team reviews each application carefully. We'll reach out within 1-2 weeks to discuss your organization and answer any questions you might have.

  • 3

    We Build Your Website

    If selected, we'll work with you to understand your needs, create content, design your site, and launch it—typically within 2-3 weeks from approval.
