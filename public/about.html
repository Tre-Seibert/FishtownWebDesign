About Us

Fishtown Web Design

Custom Web Design in Philadelphia

Hi, I'm Tre, the founder and lead developer of Fishtown Web Design. My journey into programming began when I was 12 years old, creating websites and building computers to fund my video game collection. After earning my computer science degree in May 2024 at Saint Joseph's University, here in Philadelphia, and with over two years of hands-on experience in both front-end and back-end development, I'm dedicated to delivering custom, hand-coded websites with 90+ page speed scores at an affordable starting price for small businesses.

In my experience, many small businesses in Philadelphia struggle with the upfront cost of building a new website. Those that can afford it often end up with websites that feel outdated or poorly designed, often created by developers who haven't kept up with current trends. I saw a gap where small businesses needed an affordable, trustworthy option for a performance optimized website. That's why I offer my $0 down, $150 per month plan—designed to be manageable for small businesses and to provide a custom-coded website that stands out, without breaking the bank.

Our Work Process

How We Create Performance Optimized Websites

After a contract is signed we send a small questionnaire about your business and what you offer, then we expand on that content and write the entire site's content for you, create the design, get approval, then we build. Generally takes 2-3 weeks from start to finish.

  • Questionaire

    We send you an email with less than 10 questions about you business, services, what sets you apart, etc and we do all the rest.

  • Design Approval

    We then do a video screen share showing you the first draft and make any changes you want to make sure you're 100% satisfied.

  • Development

    Once approved, we can start coding the site out and setting everything up. We will send a demo link to view before we launch and go live.
