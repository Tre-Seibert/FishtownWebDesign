About Us

Fishtown Web Design

We Make Websites With Purpose & Intent

Hi, I'm Tre, the founder and lead developer of Fishtown Web Designs. My journey into programming began when I was 12 years old, creating websites and building computers to fund my video game collection. After earning my computer science degree in May 2024 at Saint Joseph's University, here in Philadelphia, and with over two years of hands-on experience in both front-end and back-end development, I'm dedicated to delivering custom, hand-coded websites at an affordable starting price for my clients.

In my experience, many small businesses struggle with the upfront cost of building a new website. Those that can afford it often end up with websites that feel outdated or poorly designed, often created by developers who haven't kept up with current trends. I saw a gap where small businesses needed an affordable, trustworthy option for a quality website. That's why I offer my $0 down, $150 per month plan—designed to be manageable for small businesses and to provide a website that stands out, without breaking the bank.

Our Work Process

How To Get Started

After a contract is signed we send a small questionnaire about your business and what you offer, then we expand on that content and write the entire site’s content for you, create the design, get approval, then we build. Generally takes 2-3 weeks from start to finish.

  • Questionaire

    We send you an email with less than 10 questions about you business, services, what sets you apart, etc and we do all the rest.

  • Design Approval

    We then do a video screen share showing you the first draft and make any changes you want to make sure you’re 100% satisfied.

  • Development

    Once approved, we can start coding the site out and setting everything up. We will send a demo link to view before we launch and go live.
